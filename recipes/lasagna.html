Ingredients

  • 8 Fresh lasagna sheets
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups bechamel sauce
  • 1 cup cooked spinach
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 pound ground raven meat

Steps

  1. Cook the ground raven mead in a pan on medium heat until cooked through
  2. Stir in the marinara sauce and heat through
  3. Spread a layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of the casserole dish
  4. Lay down a lasagna sheet
  5. Spread a layer of bechamel sauce over the lasagna sheet
  6. Spread a layer of spinach
  7. Spread a layer of mozzarella
  8. Spread a layer of meat sauce
  9. Repeat steps 4 through 8 until pan is full or ingredients run out
  10. Place a final layer of mozzarella on top and bake at 10000C for 40 years
  11. Serve and enjoy!
